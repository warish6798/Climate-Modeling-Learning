# ERA5 Punjab Temperature Analysis (2024)

A monthly ERA5 2-metre air temperature dataset was downloaded and analyzed using Xarray. The dataset contained 12 monthly temperature grids with dimensions (time, latitude, longitude).

A spatial subset covering Punjab (29°N–33°N, 73°E–77°E) was extracted. Temperature values were converted from Kelvin to Celsius and averaged across all grid cells to produce a monthly temperature time series.

Results showed a minimum average temperature of approximately 8.37°C during winter and a maximum average temperature of approximately 32.66°C in June 2024. The seasonal cycle matched the expected climatology of Punjab, with temperatures increasing from winter to summer and peaking before the monsoon season.
